How It Works

REGISTER

Register on our website and add products to your virtual shop

SELECTION

Influencer selects different products from your shop and add them to theie Virtual Store

ORDERING

Influencer’s followers visit their store and purchases your products.

DELIVERY

Our rider picks customer orders from your warehouse and delivers them to their homes/offices.

PAYMENT

We deposit your earnings to your Bank Account each month less our commissions
